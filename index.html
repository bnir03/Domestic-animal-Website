About Domestic Animals

Domestic animals are animals that have been tamed and kept by humans for work, companionship, or food. Examples include dogs, cats, cows, and horses.

Dog: Loyal and friendly, dogs are known as man's best friend.

Cat: Independent and curious, cats make great companions.

Cow: Gentle and useful, cows provide milk and help in farming.

Horse: Strong and fast, horses have been used for transport and work.
